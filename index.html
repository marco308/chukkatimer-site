Everything for the field

From practice chukkas
to match day.

Whether you're umpiring a club game or keeping time for your team, ChukkaTimer has exactly what you need — and nothing in the way.

01

Simple timer

Start a 7-minute chukka with one tap. Audible bells at the 30-second warning and the end of the chukka — just like on the field.

02

Full game mode

Set up a complete match: team names and colours, multiple chukkas, and score tracking chukka by chukka with full history.

03

Club chukkas

Run informal club sessions with rotating Whites & Blacks sides. The live board shows who's on now and who's up next — nobody misses their chukka.

04

Automatic handicaps

ChukkaTimer calculates the HPA handicap goal start for you, and folds it into the live score. No mental arithmetic mid-match.

05

Live sharing & QR

Share a link or QR code and friends, family, and spectators follow the match live in any browser. No app required for viewers.

06

Live Activities

Keep the countdown on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island. Glance down and see the time left without unlocking.

07

Bell sounds

Choose between three different bell sounds, and set the chukka length (1–15 min) and extra time (0–60 sec) to match your club.

08

Works offline

No account, no sign-up, no data collected. Everything runs on your phone, so it just works — even where the signal doesn't.

Simple timer

One tap. Seven minutes. Go.

The fastest way to time a chukka. Big, glanceable digits you can read across the field, with the warning and end-of-chukka bells handled for you.

  • Start, pause, and reset with a tap
  • Automatic 30-second warning bell
  • Extra time runs on after the chukka ends
Game mode

Run the whole match.

Keep your matches in one place. Each game holds its teams, chukkas, and running score, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.

  • Multiple games saved on device
  • Score tracked chukka by chukka
  • Set up a new match in seconds
Teams & handicaps

Colours, handicaps — all sorted.

Name your teams, pick their colours, and set each side's handicap. ChukkaTimer works out the goal start and shows the adjusted score at a glance.

  • Team names and shirt colours
  • Automatic handicap goal start
  • Configurable chukkas, length, and extra time
Club chukkas

Built for club play,
not just matches.

Club chukkas don't fit a fixed two-team fixture — sides are picked fresh each chukka, Whites and Blacks, from a rotating roster. ChukkaTimer runs the session and broadcasts a live board, so the whole club stays in sync.

live.chukkatimer.com/saturday-club-chukkas

No scores to chase — just the time, the chukka number, and exactly who's on. Everyone at the club opens the same board on their phone.

A.

Rotating sides

Whites and Blacks are set per chukka — the line-up that changes from chukka to chukka is the data.

B.

Now & up next

A clear board shows who's on the pitch right now and who's up next, so the next four can start tacking up.

C.

Find me

Pin your name and the board highlights you — "on now", "up next", or "done" — no squinting at a paper sheet.

D.

From your chukka sheet

Built around how clubs already work — players down the rows, chukkas across the top, W / B in each cell.

The quiet part

No account. No tracking.
Just polo.

Data

Nothing collected

The App Store privacy label says it all: ChukkaTimer collects nothing about you. No analytics, no identifiers.

Account

Nothing to sign up for

Open the app and start timing. No email, no password, no onboarding between you and the first chukka.

Network

Works offline

Timer and game mode run entirely on device. Live sharing is the only thing that uses the network — and only when you choose to.

On your Lock Screen

A Live Activity keeps the countdown on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island, so you can set the phone down on the pony lines and still see exactly how long is left in the chukka.
